Emergency situation safety tips

    If firing occurs while you are at work or walking on a street:
  1. Lie down onto the ground immediately.
  2. Do not show any unwanted curiosity. The criminal may feel threatened and shoot at you.
  3. When possible, move to a safer place, slowly, without drawing attention to yourself. Take cover behind solid objects like a car or a wall.
  4. If you can see the criminals from your hiding place, try to note and memorize their distinguishing features.
  5. If you are out of sight of the criminals, call up 100 and inform the Police. If you are within earshot, try to remember their conversation.
  6. Do not disturb the scene of the crime.
